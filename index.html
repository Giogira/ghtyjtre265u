Anti-Money Laundering Policy

The ExBox.com service warns users against attempting to use the service to launder funds obtained from crime, finance terrorism, illegal actions of any kind, as well as from using the service to purchase prohibited goods and services. Money laundering is the process of concealing the illicit source of funds by converting them into funds or investments that appear legitimate.

AML and KYC procedure

1. The service carries out AML and KYC verification of incoming transactions according to its own algorithms.

2. AML and KYC verification includes the use of specialized services for analyzing cryptocurrency transactions for the presence of funds that have high risks for the Service.

3. If the analysis of an incoming transaction reveals that the permissible values ​​are exceeded (the values ​​are indicated at the bottom of this AML and KYC Policy), the funds are subject to additional analysis:

4. If additional analysis reveals a connection between the funds received and money laundering or criminal activity:

4.1. The application is suspended.

4.2. By mail, the User is requested to provide documents, photos/videos, and explanations of the origin of funds.

4.3. Upon successful verification, the funds are returned to the client to the agreed address, minus a 5% commission, but not more than the equivalent of $1000.

5. The User undertakes to provide the requested photo/video materials, wait for a verification decision and not make any claims to the Service.

6. If, based on the results of the analysis of AML-cryptocurrency assets, a risk higher than 60% is identified, or any of the following labels are received: DARK SERVICE, SCAM, STOLEN, MIXING(MIXER), SEXTORTION, RANSOMWARE, HACK, PHISHING, TERRORISM FINANCING, FRAUD, BLACKLIST, STILL UNDER INVESTIGATION, CYBERCRIME ORGANIZATION, NARCOTICS, CHILD ABUSE, HUMAN TRAFFICKING, SANCTIONS, then the user's transaction may be blocked to complete identity verification (KYC).

7. The service reserves the right to provide competent authorities with information collected during the AML/KYC procedure upon official request.

Responsible for compliance with the AML Policy

8. The AML Policy Enforcement Officer is an ExBox.com employee whose responsibilities include ensuring compliance with the AML Policy, namely:

— collection of User identification information and transfer it to the responsible agent for the processing of personal data;

— creating and updating internal policies and procedures for writing, reviewing, submitting and maintaining all reports required by existing laws and regulations;

— monitoring transactions and analyzing any significant deviations from the normal activities of Users;

— implementation of a records management system for storing and retrieving documents, files, forms and journals;

— regular updating of risk assessments.

9. The person responsible for compliance with the AML Policy has the right to interact with law enforcement agencies involved in preventing money laundering, terrorist financing and other illegal activities.

10. The service reserves the right to appoint a third-party service provider to perform the AML/KYC procedure.
